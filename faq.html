Daily Mission

1. The user's execution of daily tasks ( 1 set of tasks = 35-55 App Data) will be randomly assigned by the system based on the amount of Total Balance in the user's account with the stipulation that the App Data to be optimized cannot be cancelled or skipped during the execution of the task.

2. Total Balance amount is higher the higher the value of randomly assigned App Data and therefore the higher the profit.

3. The user will be randomly assigned App Data of different earnings types, i.e. general task and combined tasks by the system during the execution of daily tasks.

4. The combined tasks usually consist of 2-3 App Data accumulations, and all VIP level users have a chance to get a (2-in-1) combined task when they perform the daily tasks (random probability).

5. During the execution of the daily tasks, once the user get a combined task (3-in-1) consisting of 3 App Data accumulations will trigger the incentive mechanism and thus receive a bonus ( 15% Total Balance of the account).
(Negative portfolio assignments are randomly assigned by the system against the principal, usually it is 30%-250% of the principal)

Deposit Instructions

1. Setupad promises that any type of deposits made on the platform once the user has completed the daily tasks will be returned to the account and can be cashed in full without any deductions.

2. The user may decide the amount of the deposit for reset tasks (Min USD 75), depending on financial ability or familiarity with the platform.

3. The user must be recharged based on the amount of the work account Points Balance when performing a combined task.

4. The user must apply to the Online Customer Service before making a deposit in order to get a valid Crypto Address and to avoid loss of funds during the deposit process.

5. The user must provide the Online Customer Service with a proof of successful transfer after making a deposit to ensure that the funds are instantly transferred to the working account.

Withdrawal Instructions

1. Setupad will take all measures reasonably necessary to ensure that the user's funds are safely treated and will provide the user with effective advice.

• Setupad promises to provide users with real App Data after strict scrutiny to perform App Data Optimization tasks, for which users must strictly observe confidentiality with respect to their job duties.

2. The user must confirm that daily tasks have been completed before requesting to cash out all funds in the work account.

3. The user must confirm that the correct Crypto Address is linked in the FINANCIAL INFORMATION section of the working account before requesting a cash out

4. The user must Withdraw all funds from the account at the time of cash out.

5. The user must check if the funds have arrived within 30 minutes of completing the cash out and contact Online Customer Service immediately for advice if there are any problems.
- Users must complete the tasks within the time limit of the platform, if the stagnation time is too long, the credit score of their account will be affected and continue to decline

NOTE:

• The user must have completed the daily tasks within 10:00-21:59 of the platform's operating hours, otherwise the cash out will be rejected.

• The user levels VIP 1, VIP 2 & VIP 3 max daily cash out is $9,999, VIP 4 & VIP 5 no max limit.

• The user must ensure that the work account maintains a good credit score (80+) in order to cash out the full amount.
